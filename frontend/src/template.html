What is ASH? Proof-of-burned-compute token on Bittensor EVM. The only way to mint it is to solve keccak-256 puzzles and submit proof-of-work shares.
How it works Each 10-minute epoch has a share pool. Miners submit nonces, the contract verifies on-chain. At flip: claim your pro-rata share of 50 ASH.
Supply 21 million cap. Halvings every 210 000 epochs (~4 years). No admin key. No upgrades. Frozen at deploy — code is law.
